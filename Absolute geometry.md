[[Geometry]] which depends only on the first four of [[Euclid]]'s [[postulate]]s:

1. A straight [[line segment]] may be drawn between any two [[point]]s.
2. Any straight line segment may be extended indefinitely so it becomes a [[line]].
3. A [[circle]] may be drawn with any given point as [[centre of circle|centre]] and any straight line segment as [[radius]].
4. All [[right angle]]s are [[congruent]].
