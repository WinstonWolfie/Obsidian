In [[susy]], the primary way to make [[sulfur]] [[trioxide]] in [[steam age]] is from [[roaster|roasting]] [[iron sulfate]]. In [[LV]], roasting [[potassium bisulfate]] ([[programmed circuit]] #1) yields [[potassium sulfate]], [[dense steam]] and sulfur trioxide. Roasting potassium sulfate ([[programmed circuit]] #1) yields [[potash]] and sulfur trioxide. In [[MV]], it can be made from [[sulfur dioxide]] and [[oxygen]] in a roaster, with [[vanadium pentoxide]] (unlocked in MV) as catalyst.
