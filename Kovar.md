Kovar is an alloy of [[iron]], [[nickel]], and [[cobalt]]. It has a similar thermal expansion coefficient as [[glass]], making it suitable for metal to glass bonds for [[vacuum tube]]s.

In [[susy]], you can make kovar in [[primitive age]], by putting iron, nickel and [[cobalt matte]] in a 8:4:4 ratio in a [[primitive smelter]] to get 8 kovar ingot. Note all three materials need to be made in a [[primitive blast furnace]]. In [[LV]], when you get an [[electric resistence furnace]], you can smelt kovar dust with [[nitrogen]] to get kovar ingot ([[programmed circuit]] #2). Kovar dust, however, requires a [[mixer]] with the three materials in dust form and in 10:5:3 ratio. This is more resource-efficient, but cobalt dust could be a bit more difficult to process, namely from [[cobaltite]].
