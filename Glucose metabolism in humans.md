[[Glucose]] metabolism involves:

- [[glycolysis]]: breakdown of glucose to [[pyruvate]], producing [[ATP]] and [[NADH]]
- [[glycogenesis]]: conversion of glucose to [[glycogen]] for storage in the [[liver]] and [[muscles]]s
- [[glycogenolysis]]: breakdown of glycogen back to glucose during [[fasting]] etc
- [[gluconeogenesis]]: synthesis of glucose from non-[[carbohydrate]] sources, eg, [[amino acid]]s, in the [[liver]] (primary) and [[kidney]].
