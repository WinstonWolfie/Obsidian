The sum of reciprocals of [[triangular number]]s equals 2.

## Proof

$$
\frac 2 {n(n+1)}=({1\over n} - {1\over {n+1}}) \cdot 2
$$

$$
\begin{align}
\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}({1\over n} - {1\over {n+1}})&=\left( 1-\frac{1}{2} \right)+\left( \frac{1}{2}-\frac{1}{3} \right)+\dots+\left( \frac{1}{n}-\frac{1}{n+1} \right) \\
&=\lim_{ n \to \infty } (1-\frac{1}{n+1})=1.
\end{align}
$$
