In [[susy]], steel is a crucial material to kick-start the [[industrialisation]]. In [[primitive age]], making steel requires a [[primitive blast furnace]], [[carburising|carburised]] from [[wrought iron]]. This will be the main method to get steel before [[electric resistence furnace]]. After, [[pig iron]] can be smelted with [[quicklime]] in a 10:1 ratio, with [[oxygen]] and [[programmed circuit]] #1.

In real life, steel is an alloy of [[iron]] and [[carbon]] (usually not exceeding 2%).
