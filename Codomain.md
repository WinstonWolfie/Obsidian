A [[set]] within which the values of a [[function]] lie (as opposed to the [[range]], which is the set of values that the function actually takes). ([[Wolfram MathWorld]])

That is to say, for any function $f : X \to Y,$ the range ([[image]]) of $f$ is always a [[subset]] of the codomain $Y.$ It may be a [[proper subset]] or it may equal the codomain.
