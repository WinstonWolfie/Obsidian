[[Euler]]'s totient [[function]] answers the question: How many [[integer]]s (less than or equal to $x$) are [[relatively prime]] to $x?$ For example, $\varphi(10)=4,$ $\varphi(15)=8.$

Note that for any [[prime number]], $\varphi(p)=p-1.$
