In [[Susy]], ebonite plate is used to make [[lead-acid battery|lead-acid batteries]] in [[LV]]. Ebonite is made from [[latex]], [[sulfur]] and [[carbon]], similar to [[rubber]].

In real life, ebonite is a brand name for a material generically known as hard rubber or vulcanite, obtained via [[sulfur vulcanisation|vulcanising]] [[natural rubber]] for prolonged periods.
