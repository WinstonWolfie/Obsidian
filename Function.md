A function is a relation that uniquely associates [[element|members]] of one [[set]] with members of another set. ([[Wolfram MathWorld]])

Examples of functions over the [[real number|reals]] include $\sin x$ ([[many-to-one]]), $x$ ([[one-to-one]]), $x^2$ (two-to-one except for the single point $x = 0$), etc.
